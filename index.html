This is a wargame simulation. The scenario is that the enemy tanks are invading. Your goal is to destroy the red tanks, not the blue ones.

Red tanks are easily identifiable as they are always facing left. They use dual tracks or hover technology. Their identifier is a black X.

Blue Tanks are easily identifiable as they are always facing right. They use single tracks or wheels. Their identifier is a white upside-down triangle.  

Destroying red tanks will increase your score, and destroying blue tanks lowers your health. You have 60 seconds to destroy 20 enemy tanks with 5 health.
